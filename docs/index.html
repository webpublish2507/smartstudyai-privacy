SmartStudyAI Privacy Policy

Your study data, clearly explained.

This Privacy Policy explains how SmartStudyAI collects, uses, stores, and protects user information when you use the mobile application. SmartStudyAI is an academic support app that helps students manage scores, generate study schedules, create quizzes, and interact with AI-powered study assistance.

Effective date: April 18, 2026
Package name: com.studysmartai.app
Platform: Android

1. Information We Collect

SmartStudyAI may collect and store the following information based on how the app is used:

  • Subject names, score records, quiz results, schedules, and study tasks entered by the user.
  • Chat messages and history created inside the Study Companion feature.
  • Images or files selected by the user for AI-based explanation features.
  • Basic device-generated technical data that may be used by the Android system or Google Play services.

2. How We Use Data

The information collected is used only to provide the functions of the app, including:

  • Saving and displaying academic records and study planning data.
  • Generating quizzes, feedback, and study support content.
  • Supporting AI chat and AI explanation features through the Gemini API.
  • Improving user experience inside the app.

3. AI Services and Third-Party Processing

SmartStudyAI uses the Gemini API for live AI-related features. When these features are used, user prompts, selected text, and uploaded content may be sent to Google for processing in order to generate AI responses.

SmartStudyAI does not train a custom AI model. It uses an external AI API to generate quiz, schedule, and companion responses.

Users should avoid uploading highly sensitive personal information through AI features unless necessary for academic use.

4. Local Storage

Most user-generated data, such as scores, schedules, settings, saved subjects, quiz history, and chat history, is stored locally on the device using the app’s local storage system.

  • Data remains on the device unless the user deletes it or clears the app data.
  • Uninstalling the app may remove locally stored app data from the device.

5. Data Sharing

SmartStudyAI does not sell user data. User content is only shared with third-party services when required to provide app functions, such as sending prompts or uploaded content to the Gemini API for AI-generated responses.

6. Data Security

We aim to protect user information through reasonable technical measures within the limits of a student project application. However, no digital system can guarantee absolute security.

7. Children’s Privacy

SmartStudyAI is designed for students and general academic use. It is not intentionally designed to collect personal information from children under the age required by local law without appropriate supervision.

8. User Rights and Deletion

Users may manage or remove information by:

  • Deleting scores, tasks, quiz history, and chat history inside the app.
  • Clearing app data from Android device settings.
  • Uninstalling the app from the device.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any updated version will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
